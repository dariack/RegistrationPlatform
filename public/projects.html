HUJI Hackathon 2016 Projects
Making Jerusalem Smarter

citibot
1st Place Winner

A smart communication channel between the municipality and the residents. The resident writes a message on the municipality's facebook page and a smart bot responses quickly and efficiently. The system is also able to update the user about developments on the topic.

Technologies: wit.ai, Meteor, react

ArtZion
2nd Place Winner

A project including digital exhibitions located all around the city on smart points with screens and speakers. Artists can upload their artworks on the website for it to be showed, and an app enables people passing by to influence the art displayed in real-time.

thermote
3rd Place Winner

A platform that allows monitoring and affecting the temperature in public places using a sensor and electronic device. Users can vote in the app and a comfortable temperature that satisfies the most is calculated.

Technologies: arduino, NodeJS, JAVA, RapidAPI, 3D printing, EasyIOT, Android

bikeep

A docking system where any biker can store his bike in without worrying about it being stolen. The dock can charge your e-bike and communicates with your mobile phone to reserve a place, to monitor and to get notifications about whether anyone tried to tamper with your bike.

Technologies: Android, bluetooth system-on-chip

BUZR

An app that turns the city into a marketplace by allowing small businesses to publish immediate, short term, localized, special deals and offering them to consumers while they are in the neraby area.

Technologies: Android, Java, Picasso, Material Design, Python, Flask, SQLite

MiSham

Geographically-centered citizen polls initiated by the city municipality.

HOLLYFOOD

Helps the user find his next favorite place to eat in the city. The system uses the user's opinion about places to help him find new places that he would like. In a city with almost 1000 restaurants, HOLLYFOOD can help you navigate your way in a world of endless culinary options.

Technologies: Microsoft Azure, Ubunto VM, JavaScript, HTML, JQuery

PLAYGROUND

A fitness application that creates personalized workout routes for the user in his area in the city. The app suggest routes for running, cycling or a strength workout.

Technologies: Android, Python, Google Maps API, Jerusalem GIS, Tornado, Microsoft Azure

Kehila

A map-based app aspiring to connect people to the community around them, by viewing events near by, reporting hazards and communicating with the minicipality.

Technologies: LAMP, JS, OSM

Getlocal

A web app matching you a local guide, tailored to your interests. Harnessing the unconventional knowledge of local experts for creating a global community, making international tourism much more interesting and accessible.

Technologies: RapidAPI, Bootstrap, JQuery, Ajax, CSS3

Second City

The app allows users to reimagine the city and share their ideas with the world. A user can place pictures, drawings, sounds and videos as virtual objects in the ‘second city’ - and they will be visible via the app for all other users when they’re in the same place in the regular city.

Technologies: OpenCV, MongoDB, Google Cloud, WikiTude

JERUSALERT

An app allowing residents to see the reports others have passed to the municipality on a map. Users can upload new reports, or rate and update the existing ones.

SNITCH

A tool developed for the Israel police, using OrCam's MyMe, the first wearable AI device.

Technologies: Orcam MyMe

Cup O'Sugar

The app helps people to connect in the neighborhood, share groceries and be more socialized and friendly with their neighbors. The user enters groceries he can offer and the app finds him the nearest neighbors that have the things he needs.

TeamMate

An app for finding team mates for sport games or workouts. The user can join an exiting group or create a new one.

Go Fresh

A social map-based app that connects farmers to private consumers of fresh fruit and vegtables. Posts uploaded on the app last only 72 hours in order to guarantee freshness.

Technologies: Android, GPS

ParkIt

An application that helps people find a parking space in Jerusalem. The user asks for a parking spot in a specific area and can choose a preferred type of parking space. The app uses the minicipality database and other users' inputs.

Technologies: Android, PHP, mySQL

tofsy

An Android app that serves as a convenient tool for bureaucracy task involving form filling. The app organizes the required forms for various tasks and helps quickly filling and submitting them.

Technologies: Android

TRAMPICK

An app for sharing tremps around the city and creating a car pools for your everyday activities. A safe and friendly community allowing to offer or join rides in this social, eco-friendly, gasoline saving app.

Technologies: Android

Localeyez

Visit the city as a local - the app creates a city tour for the user according to his intrests. The user can also ratethe sites and add new ones to the app. It also connects tourists with locals that volunteered to tourguide in their area.

Technologies: Google Firebase

pocketour

An app that provides information on sites and building while touring the city. Once you take a picture of a place you get relevant information, pictures and an audio explanations.

Technologies: Android, SQLite, GPS, Image Processing (OpenCV), WebScraping (Scrapy)

the Regularization Geese

A system for nline traffic flow optimization - models, simulates and minimizes waiting-time in junctions (with traffic lights). The algorithm solves an online (batch) convex optimization problem, where the goal function is the minimization of cars waiting-time.

Technologies: Python, MATLAB

lend & borrow

Allow people in the same neighbourhoods or people living near each other to share stuff between them for short periods of time, without the need to pay anything.
